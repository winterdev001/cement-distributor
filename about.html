About Us

Kiku Cement Distributors Co, is a locally registered sole proprietorship firm based in Kigali-Rwanda, which is working as distributor/wholesaler, supplier and trader of best good quality construction cement. We provide top notch brands of cement that contribute much in construction projects.

Our cements are of high grade as it plays a big role when it comes to harsh weather condition and environment instability. For construction it is recommended to choose and use best quality cement and that is why our company is been serving the country since 2010 and we are a good source to depend on as our top brands of cement includes- Tanzania Portland Cement, Nyati Cement, Dangote Cement, Bamburi Cement, Mombasa Cement, Derba MIDROC Cement, Mugher Cement, etc.
